Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)

Uses your own blood to tell your skin to repair itself.

Duration45 minutes FromConsultation required Performed byNurse Tracey

What it is

PRP uses the platelets and plasma from your own blood to stimulate growth factors in your tissue. Nothing synthetic goes in.

Who it suits

Wrinkles, sun damage and scarring. It is also used to treat hair thinning and hair loss.

What a course looks like

Assessed individually.

What you can expect

  • Stimulated tissue repair
  • Improved sun damage and scarring
  • Also treats hair thinning

A consultation with a registered nurse is required before this treatment to assess your suitability. Individual results vary and are not guaranteed. Any procedure carries risks, which will be discussed with you.

Pricing

Consultation required. Price varies.

These treatments are prescribed, not purchased. What you need depends on your face, and quoting a number before Tracey has assessed you would be a guess.
