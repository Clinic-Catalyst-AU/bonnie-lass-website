Bio-Stimulation

Preserves the collagen you have and prompts your cells to make more.

Duration30 minutes FromConsultation required Performed byNurse Tracey

What it is

Stimulates cell activity to increase collagen and elastin production, preserving the skin's existing collagen while building new.

Who it suits

Low-laxity skin that needs quality, structure and firming. Face, neck, decolletage, hands, knees and abdomen.

What a course looks like

Minimum of 3 treatments, four to six weeks apart, with strict adherence to aftercare.

What you can expect

  • Increased collagen and elastin
  • Improved structure and firmness
  • Better overall skin quality

A consultation with a registered nurse is required before this treatment to assess your suitability. Individual results vary and are not guaranteed. Any procedure carries risks, which will be discussed with you.

Pricing

Consultation required. Price varies.

These treatments are prescribed, not purchased. What you need depends on your face, and quoting a number before Tracey has assessed you would be a guess.
