Fine Line & Wrinkle Treatments

Softens the lines you have and slows the ones on their way.

Duration30 minutes FromConsultation required Performed byNurse Tracey

What it is

Relaxes the facial muscles that fold the skin, softening existing lines and slowing the formation of new ones. Results typically last 3 to 4 months, with longer-lasting options available.

Who it suits

Assessed individually. Pricing depends on the treatment area and the product used. A consultation with our registered nurse is required to assess your suitability, and some procedures require a pre-treatment video call with a cosmetic doctor.

What a course looks like

Typically every 3 to 4 months.

What you can expect

  • Softened existing lines
  • Prevention of new lines
  • Assessed and prescribed individually

A consultation with a registered nurse is required before this treatment to assess your suitability. Individual results vary and are not guaranteed. Any procedure carries risks, which will be discussed with you.

Pricing

Consultation required. Price varies by area.

These treatments are prescribed, not purchased. What you need depends on your face, and quoting a number before Tracey has assessed you would be a guess.
