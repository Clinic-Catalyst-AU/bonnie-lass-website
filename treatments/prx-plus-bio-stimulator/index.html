What it is

PRX-Plus stimulates collagen deep in the dermis without breaking the skin's surface. There is no needling and no peeling, so there is no downtime - you can have it done and go back to work.

Who it suits

Skin that has lost firmness and radiance but is not ready for anything invasive. It is safe for all skin types and all seasons, which most resurfacing treatments are not.

What a course looks like

3 to 5 sessions, two weeks apart.

What you can expect

  • Instant glow
  • Long-term firming
  • No peeling
  • No downtime
