Volume Restoring

Puts back the structure that went missing, in the places it actually went.

Duration30 to 45 minutes FromConsultation required Performed byNurse Tracey

What it is

Adds structure and volume to facial structures, hydrating and enhancing your natural features. Typical areas include cheeks, chin, nasolabial folds, peri-oral, marionettes, tear troughs, jawline and pan-facial.

Who it suits

Faces that have deflated - most commonly after significant weight loss, or through the natural fat-pad loss of the forties and fifties.

What a course looks like

Assessed individually. A consultation with a registered nurse is required.

What you can expect

  • Restored facial structure
  • Enhanced natural features
  • Rested rather than 'done'

A consultation with a registered nurse is required before this treatment to assess your suitability. Individual results vary and are not guaranteed. Any procedure carries risks, which will be discussed with you.

Pricing

Consultation required. Price varies.

These treatments are prescribed, not purchased. What you need depends on your face, and quoting a number before Tracey has assessed you would be a guess.
